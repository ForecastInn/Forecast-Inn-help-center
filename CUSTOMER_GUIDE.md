## 3. Fitur Prediksi AI

### Cara Pakai:

1. **Buka menu "AI Forecasting"**
2. **Pilih tanggal range** (misal: 1 - 30 hari ke depan)
3. **Klik "Generate Forecast"**
4. **Lihat hasil:**
   - Prediksi occupancy per hari
   - Estimasi revenue
   - Rekomendasi harga

### Tips:
- ✅ Jalankan forecast minimal **seminggu sekali**
- ✅ Bandingkan forecast dengan actual
- ✅ Adjust pricing berdasarkan rekomendasi AI

## 4. Analisis Revenue

### Metrics yang Tersedia:

1. **RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room)**
   - Formula: Total Revenue / Total Kamar Tersedia
   - Target: > market benchmark

2. **GOPPAR (Gross Operating Profit Per Available Room)**
   - Profitabilitas per kamar
   - Target: Lihat budget hotel

3. **Market Penetration**
   - Perbandingan vs kompetitor
   - Target: >100% (above market)

### Cara Export Report:
1. Pilih date range
2. Klik **"Export Report"**
3. Pilih format: PDF / Excel / CSV

## 5. Manajemen Booking

### Cek Booking:

1. **Buka menu "Bookings"**
2. **Filter berdasarkan:**
   - Date range (arrival/departure)
   - Status (confirmed, pending, cancelled)
   - Room type
   - Channel (OTA, direct, dll)

### Cancel/Modify Booking:

1. **Cari booking** di list
2. **Klik booking ID**
3. **Pilih action:** Cancel / Modify dates / Change room
4. **Klik "Confirm"**

## 6. Tips & Best Practices

### 💡 **Optimalkan Revenue:**

1. **Dynamic Pricing**
   - Adjust harga berdasarkan demand
   - Tingkatkan harga saat high occupancy
   - Promo saat low demand

2. **Length of Stay (LOS) Restrictions**
   - Minimum night saat peak season
   - Maksimum night untuk long-stay guests

3. **Channel Management**
   - Prioritaskan direct booking (lebih profitable)
   - Monitor OTA commission
   - Adjust OTA availability saat full

### 📅 **Daily Routine (5 Menit):**

1. Cek **forecast hari ini**
2. Review **new bookings**
3. Adjust **pricing** jika perlu
4. Cek **cancellations**

### 📆 **Weekly Review (30 Menit):**

1. Review **performance minggu lalu**
2. Compare **vs forecast**
3. Update **forecast 30 hari ke depan**
4. Adjust **strategy** untuk bulan depan
